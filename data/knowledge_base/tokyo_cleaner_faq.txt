Tokyo Cleaner — Common Customer Questions & Inquiries

📍 Service Area & Booking Logistics
Service Area Coverage
Customers often ask whether their property is within our service area.


Our main service area covers:
Minato-ku
Chiyoda-ku
Chuo-ku
Shinjuku-ku
Shibuya-ku


In some cases, we may accept properties outside these wards if travel time is within or less than approximately 45–50 minutes one way.


Please note that our service is currently available only for properties located within a 15-minute walk from the nearest station, to ensure smooth logistics and efficient staff access.
Booking Payment Status
Customers may ask:
Whether payment was completed successfully
Whether their booking is confirmed and asked for us to resend receipt.
Unpaid bookings may be automatically cancelled after 2 days.
Same-Day Service
Same-day bookings may be possible depending on:
Staff availability
Schedule/logistics for the day
Future Bookings
Customers sometimes request trial or recurring cleaning far in advance.
Advance booking is possible if:
The schedule is open
Staff availability can be secured
Scheduling Changes
Requests to change confirmed cleaning dates or times are common.
Changes may be possible depending on staff availability.

🧼 Cleaning Scope & Special Requests
Focus Areas
Customers commonly request focus on:
Kitchen / range
Bathroom
Floors
Closet organization/help
Specialized or Additional Tasks
Some customers may request additional or non-standard cleaning services, including:
Organizing / Decluttering
 We can assist with organizing and tidying the space.


Disposal Assistance
We can help separate trash and place waste in the building’s designated garbage area.  However, we are unable to remove or take trash off-site.


Balcony Cleaning
Balcony cleaning is considered labor-intensive and requires additional booking time.
This service is charged as an additional 1-hour service (JPY 4,800).


Window Cleaning
Window cleaning may incur additional charges depending on the room size and condition.


Bed Making / Linen Setup
This service is possible if clean linens and covers are prepared in advance by the customer.


Deep Cleaning Service
Deep cleaning is classified as labor-intensive work.
The standard additional fee is JPY 22,000 for 2 hours with 2 staff members.
**However, recent photos of the room condition may be required before confirmation.
Note: Services involving laundry time, extensive setup, large window cleaning, or labor-intensive work may require additional charges.

Language Preference
