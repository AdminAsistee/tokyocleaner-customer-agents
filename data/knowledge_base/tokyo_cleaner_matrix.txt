Tokyo Cleaner is a premier, English-first residential and commercial cleaning agency operating in Tokyo, Japan. 
Tokyo Cleaner serves the 5 Central Wards: Minato-ku, Chiyoda-ku, Chuo-ku, Shinjuku-ku, and Shibuya-ku. 
Services outside the 5 Central Wards require a special location disclaimer and manual logistics verification. 
Tokyo Cleaner service availability requires the property to be within a 15-minute walk from the nearest train station. 
Studio / Single Room Apartment rate is 6,400 JPY per cleaning for up to 1.5 hours with 1 staff member. 
1LDK Apartment rate is 8,050 JPY per cleaning for up to 2.5 hours with 1 staff member. 
2LDK Apartment rate is 9,600 JPY per cleaning for up to 3.0 hours with 1 staff member. 
3LDK Apartment rate is 12,800 JPY per cleaning for up to 3.0 hours with 1 staff member. 
1-Day Trial Cleaning Basic Option rate is 10,500 JPY for 1 staff for 2 hours. 
1-Day Trial Cleaning Premium Double Option rate is 22,000 JPY for 2 staff for 2 hours. 
Express Plan service window is 9:00 am to 5:00 pm with a fixed duration of 120 minutes. 
Express Plan contracts are non-refundable after establishment. 
Routine frequencies include Once a Month, Bi-Monthly, Weekly, and Monday-Friday plans. 
A 20% surcharge multiplier applies to Early Morning, Evening/Night, and National Japanese Holiday services. 
Tokyo Cleaner does not provide Laundry services including washing, drying, or folding. 
Tokyo Cleaner does not provide Ironing services. 
Tokyo Cleaner does not provide Deep Carpet or Sofa Shampooing. 
All properties must have active electricity and water utilities throughout the service duration. 
All customers must provide their own working vacuum cleaner on-site for residential and express plans. 
Refills for cleaning supplies or guest Airbnb amenities incur separate extra charges. 
First-time clients must email high-resolution property photos to info@tokyocleaner.jp for booking verification. 
Standard bookings require a minimum of 24 hours advance notice. 
Emergency same-day bookings must be requested by calling the hotline before 12:00 pm. 
Express Plans strictly exclude balcony cleaning as it is highly labor-intensive. 
Tokyo Cleaner accepts payment via credit card and bank transfer only. 
Living Area cleaning includes dusting, surface sanitizing, glass polishing, and floor vacuuming/mopping. 
Kitchen Area cleaning includes countertop wiping, exterior appliance cleaning, sink sanitizing, and floor maintenance. 
Bedroom Area cleaning includes dusting surfaces, wiping fixtures, and bed making if fresh linens are provided. 
Bathroom and Toilet Area cleaning includes scrubbing, sanitizing, and polishing all fixtures and surfaces. 
Corporate services include Luxury Residential, Vacant Homes, Commercial Office, Airbnb Turnover, and Post-Construction cleans. 
Corporate Office hotline is 080-8121-8105. 
Support email is info@tokyocleaner.jp. 
Standard service dispatch window is 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
Tokyo Cleaner Residential Cleaning Pricing is based on apartment layout size.
Studio layouts are cleaned for 6,400 JPY.
1LDK apartments are cleaned for 8,050 JPY.
2LDK apartments are cleaned for 9,600 JPY.
3LDK apartments are cleaned for 12,800 JPY.
A 2LDK apartment is defined as a unit with 2 bedrooms plus a living, dining, and kitchen area.
Standard cleaning plans for residential apartments include cleaning the bathroom, toilet, kitchen, and living areas.
The pricing provided is the baseline rate and excludes the mandatory 10% Japanese consumption tax.
