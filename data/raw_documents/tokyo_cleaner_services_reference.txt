4. RIGID SERVICE LIMITATIONS & HARD BOUNDARIES (CRITICAL FOR INTAKE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To manage client expectations, any AI or human intake framework must explicitly 
communicate what Tokyo Cleaner CANNOT do:

1. NO Laundry Services: The staff will not wash, dry, or fold clothes.
2. NO Ironing Services: Ironing of apparel is strictly excluded.
3. NO Deep Carpet/Sofa Shampooing: Steam cleaning or chemical deep extractions 
   for furniture, drapery, or heavy carpets are not supported.
4. Utility Requirement: Running electricity and active water utilities MUST be 
   turned on and accessible inside the unit during the entire service. (Crucial for Move-out Cleanings).
5. On-Site Vacuum Requirement: Customers are required to provide their own working 
   vacuum cleaner on-site for regular residential plans and express cleaning plans.
6. Refill Exclusions: Refills for cleaning supplies or guest amenities (for Airbnbs) 
   incur separate extra charges.
7. First-Time Room Photo Verification Policy: If a user is a first-time client, 
   they MUST email high-resolution photos of their room or property's current 
   condition to info@tokyocleaner.jp post-booking for final assessment. First-time 
   bookings must also explicitly specify the property size.
8. Short-Notice Buffer: Standard bookings require a minimum of 24 hours advance notice. 
   If a customer wishes to request emergency same-day service, they must call the hotline 
   by 12:00 pm at the latest, entirely subject to staff real-time availability.
9. NO Balcony Cleaning under Express: Balcony cleaning is strictly excluded from 
   Express Cleaning plans. Balcony cleaning requires an additional extra time booking 
   as it is highly labor-intensive.
10. Accepted Methods of Payment: Credit card and bank transfer only.

5. SERVICE TASK CHECKLIST BY AREA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Every standard service package covers specific, meticulous tasks across four 
primary residential quarters:

A. Living Area
- Tidy, dust, and thoroughly sanitize all living surfaces.
- Wipe and sanitize frequently-touched hardware: handles, plates, and light switches.
- Polish mirrors and glass fixtures.
- Empty internal trash bins and replace trash liner bags if replacements are provided.
- Complete floor maintenance: full vacuuming and wet mopping.
- Pet-friendly cleaning customizations are available strictly upon explicit request.

B. Kitchen Area
- Dust and wipe down countertops, dining tables, and seating chairs.
- Clean and sanitize exterior appliance surfaces: cabinet doors, refrigerator faces, 
  stovetops, and exterior oven structures.
- Sweep, vacuum, and mop heavy-traffic kitchen floors.
- Polish and deep-sanitize sink basin, faucet controls, and counters.
- Separate and empty trash and recycling containers (replacing liner bags).

C. Bedroom Area
- Dust and clean all accessible bedside tables, desks, and shelving surfaces.
- Wipe down and sanitize door handles, light fixtures, and wall mirrors.
- Make up the bed structure using fresh linen (linens must be placed out by the 
  client prior to the cleaner's arrival).
- Complete vacuum and floor mop sweeps.

D. Bathroom & Toilet Area
- Scrub, sanitize, and polish the toilet unit, bathtub/shower stalls, sink basins, 
  and primary mirrors.
- Wipe down cabinetry exteriors, handles, and electrical plates.
- Empty the small bathroom wastebins, refresh liners, and mop floor tilings.
